About Us

WADE (K) LTD FARM was founded in 1987 with farming interest in dairy farming. This interest has, in later and in more recent years shifted, to propagating, growing and exporting herbs. The primary cause for the shift to herbs farming is to create sustainable incomes for the investment and elevation of social economic status of the company workers and the neighboring community. The projects farm is situated in Nkaruwuani Location, of Olkinyei Group Ranch area, in Narok South district, Narok County a distance of approximately 65 kilometers to South West of Narok Town. The geographical location of the land is 10 10’ 46.2555” South of the Equator and 350 33’ 33.8944” East of the Greenwich at an altitude of 1887.320 meters above mean sea level. The farm is roughly rectangular in shape measuring 1.43 kilometers by1.02 kilometers, covering an area of 137.420 Hectares (340 Acres), with a seasonal river along one of the boundary.
